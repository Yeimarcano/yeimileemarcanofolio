BMCC TimelyTask App

Ux & UI Design | 2024 | Figma

The app is a student organizer in which you will learn how to manage your time at the moment of submitting your assignments, your study hours for your upcoming quizzes, and work on those big projects. The app has a student calendar to look for your class schedule, BMCC Calendar and events. In this way, you could see your free hours to work on your student life and personal one.

One of its most important features is the creation of reminders for assignments and exam deadlines, assigning notifications and a countdown to the due date you set for your projects. Your student projects will be arranged by categories and classes. You can even mark a specific task as important, so the main countdown on the homepage and the reminders page will focus on that important task.

    Problem

  • BMCC students feel a bit squeezed with class schedules, part-time jobs, and even extracurricular activities.
  • Procrastination.
  • Forgetting deadlines.
  • Difficulty prioritizing tasks when everything feels urgent.
  • Find it difficult to manage their time and often feel overwhelmed by deadlines.

    Goal

  • Stay on top of assignments and deadlines.
  • Balance academic work with personal projects and social life.
  • Develop better productivity habits.
  • Reduce stress

The purpose of this app is not only to keep your class schedules and BMCC calendar together but also to remember things you tend to forget. The idea behind this app is to help organize your time and become more responsible. In your reminders, the countdown of days will help you manage your time effectively for completing your assignments on time and studying for your quizzes without proscatination until the last minute.
